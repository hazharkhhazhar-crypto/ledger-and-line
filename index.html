Frequently Asked Questions

Will my resume pass ATS filters?

No one can guarantee a pass rate — it depends on the specific system a company uses and how closely your background matches the role. What I can guarantee is a format that parses cleanly (no tables, columns, or graphics that get mangled) and language aligned to the keywords in the job description you give me.

Why pay via TON?

It's the payment rail I currently have set up, and it settles fast without a payment processor taking a cut. If TON isn't practical for you, email me first — I can usually arrange an alternative.

How long does it take?

Premium Rewrites are delivered within 48 hours of your payment and files arriving. Audits are typically same-day. Standard Templates are sent within a few hours.

What do I actually receive?

A Word (.docx) and PDF version of your resume, sent to the email address you provide in the order form.

Can I get a refund?

If I haven't started work on your order, yes. Once a rewrite or audit has been delivered, it's final — email me if something's wrong and I'll fix it rather than refund it.
